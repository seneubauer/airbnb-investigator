Delivering awareness and preparation efficiency to every adventurer in America

Our mission was to construct a user-friendly map with a central focus on AirBnB hosts, their locations, metadata about what each offers, and how they compare to others. Additionally, we didn’t want to neglect other considerations a person might want to know. So as a secondary feature of the map, we included airports.

Customizable search toggles like airports had limits. We were limited by our rapidly swelling wishlist and huge volume of data as we brainstormed ideas we could include. This was further limited once we realized our own imaginations may neglect your ideas.

Instead as a tertiary feature, custom searching is offered through a google places API tool. This way no special interest is neglected. Maybe soap carvings are your thing. Did you know S. Korea now has a toilet theme park”? This map can deliver things like those.

Another feature we included is a 5 Day weather forecast. Some of the cities in America boast consistently perfect weather, like San Francisco. Others, not so much. Consider October in Fargo, ND, where it can be 68 and sunny, then deliver a foot of snow. Here, you can select your destination prior to embarking, check for surprise updates, & best prepare for your journey.

You are an intrepid soul, and so are we. And that is why we built this application. We want to spend our time wisely. We want to prepare for an adventure with simplicity. We want to efficiently research all the peculiar nuances of our proclivities, and confidently run off into the weird, wild, world that beckons us.
